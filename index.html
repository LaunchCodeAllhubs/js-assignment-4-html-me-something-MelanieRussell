DC guide for newcomers!

Movies

"DC has of all kinds to start off with! Maybe you want to watch a noir film with a darker tone like "The Batman" or maybe your in the mood for something more light and kid friendly like "Superpets"! There's even a whole Unverse to explore in the DCEU!"

Animation

"One of the things that makes DC stand out is its large selction of animated media. Many people began thier love of DC through the DCAU which includes many emmy award-winning tv series such as Batman The Animated Series, Static Shock, and Justice League: Unlimited. There is also the New 52 Movies which has brought some of DC's best comic stories to life through animation!"

Comic Books

"Many people don't know where to start when reading comics. Thats why DC comics has just started the Dawn of DC event! Many of DC's most iconic characters have started new story arcs that new readers can follow along with. If you have any questions, don't be afraid to ask your local comic book store and do your own research.
